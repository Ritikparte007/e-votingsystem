4. Voting Conduct

Each verified voter is entitled to cast exactly one vote per election. Once submitted, votes are final and cannot be changed.

By casting your vote, you confirm that you are the registered account holder and are exercising your own free democratic choice without coercion or undue influence.

Upon successful voting, a unique Vote Receipt ID will be generated. This receipt serves as proof of participation but does not reveal your candidate choice — your vote remains secret.
