Privacy Policy

We are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a voter.

Effective: April 1, 2026 Last Updated: April 14, 2026 Jurisdiction: India

Your Privacy Matters: We collect only the minimum data necessary to run secure elections. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal information during registration and use of the Platform:

Data Type What We Collect Why
Identity Full Name, Date of Birth, State Voter identification
Documents Voter ID, Aadhaar Number Eligibility verification
Contact Email, Mobile Number OTP verification, communication
Credentials Password (BCrypt encrypted) Account security
Activity Login time, IP address, actions Audit trail, security
Vote Receipt ID, timestamp Proof of participation

Vote Secrecy: Your actual candidate choice is NEVER stored in a way that links it back to your identity. Your vote remains completely secret.

2. How We Use Your Data

Identity Verification

To verify your eligibility to vote using Voter ID and Aadhaar

OTP Authentication

To send verification codes to your registered mobile number

Election Statistics

Aggregated, anonymous vote counts for public results display

Audit & Security

Activity logging for election integrity and fraud detection

3. Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored in a secured PostgreSQL database with the following protections:

BCrypt Password Hashing

All passwords are hashed with BCrypt strength-12. Plain text passwords are NEVER stored.

Active

Database Row Level Security

PostgreSQL RLS prevents unauthorized modification of vote records.

Active

Session-Based Authentication

Server-side sessions ensure only you can access your account.

Active

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

Your data may be shared only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal requirement: When required by Indian law, court order, or government authority
  • Twilio SMS: Your mobile number is shared with Twilio only to deliver OTP messages — no other data is shared
  • Public results: Aggregated, anonymous vote counts are displayed publicly — no personal data

5. Cookies & Sessions

This Platform uses server-side sessions to manage your login state. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

We Use

  • • Session cookie (login state only)
  • • Automatically deleted on logout
  • • Server-side, not readable by JS

We Do NOT Use

  • • Advertising cookies
  • • Third-party tracking
  • • Analytics cookies (Google etc.)

6. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the following rights:

Right to Access

Request a copy of your stored personal data

Right to Correction

Request correction of inaccurate data

Right to Deletion

Request deletion after election period (where legally permitted)

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured format
